Tacos

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Orégano
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Dry chili (to your liking)
  • Cilantro
  • tortillas

How to make Tacos

Here is a brief summary of how to make them

  • Cut the meat into strips, place in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
  • In a frying pan put a dash of oil, the dried chili and oregano and heat for 2 or 3 minutes.
  • Add the meat
  • Heat the rest of the vegetables until cooked
  • Heat the tortillas.
  • Preparation of tacos
  • Serve and accompany with a sauce
