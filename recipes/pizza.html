Pizza

Ingredients

  • 400 grams of wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon fresh yeast
  • 200 ml of water
  • A pinch of salt
  • A pinch of sugar
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • ham
  • pepperoni or sausage

How to make Pizza

Here is a brief summary of how to do it

  • Make the pizza dough.
  • Crush the tomatoes for the sauce.
  • Grate the mozzarella cheese
  • Chop all other ingredients
  • Pizza preparation
  • Bake in a preheated oven at 200 degrees.
  • Cook for 10-15 minutes. Watch that it does not burn
  • Once the pizza is ready, cut it into its classic triangular shape and enjoy it with the family.
