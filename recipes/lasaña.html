Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

How to make lasagna

Here is a brief summary of how to do it

  • Prepare the sauce.
  • Cook the noodles.
  • Meat preparation
  • Layer the lasagna with each of the ingredients.
  • Cover with aluminum foil and bake.
  • Let the lasagna rest before serving.
